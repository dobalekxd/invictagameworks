About Us
We are KoGaMa creators motivated to work on astonishing projects for the community. We don't call ourselves a "studio" - We don't feel it, we are not a company. We are rather just a group of friends, with the same goal and passion for creation - A "group" is more appropriate. Our group aims to push the limits of what's incredible in games. We want to make them unique, breathtaking and inspiring.
Workstyle
As we are just a group of friends, there's no really a division for "owners" and "workers". In our group everyone is equal. You join the group to work with someone, and not for someone - We simply help each other with projects!

We plan and design our games together - Everyone's opinion and ideas are taken into account! No one should feel more or less invloved in a project.

We have some tactic of splitting our work-power - We usually have one big, main project, one big, side project and some small others, which we complete in a break, or put on hold and resume for short periods. Generally, we focus on two projects at once.
