Welcome to Каруба

The soviet island veiled in mystery. There was no question of a mistake, no one could realistically explain what could have caused the sudden disappearance of the three thriving cities that had once comprised the heart of the archipelago renowned for its industrial advancements. You find yourself amidst the ruins of the tiniest among the three towns— or rather, what remains of it. You see no civilians, only remnants of their presence.

Post-Apocalyptic Soviet Reality

Desolate stores, apartments, ruined buildings, vestiges of protests and rejected propaganda. The place is shrouded in catastrophe. The precise events remain undisclosed, but can be aptly described with the word of terror.

Deep, Immersive World

Every step you take unveils a hidden tale of darkness that shrouds every place traversed. There is no a shadow of life in sight, only lingering echoes of the past. Countless structures and locations bear the ghastly resemblance of authentic landmarks from Chernobyl or Pripyat. Each of these meticulously crafted sites holds within it a profound essence, immersing you in the authentic aura of Soviet architecture, evoking a powerful connection to a bygone era.

Gameplay

Two opposite teams control two halves of the city - The residental and industrial districts. Both teams can unlock various customizations of their half of the map that will help them in the battle. Those might be defensive objects, parkour trails, hideouts, tactical spots or sniping positions.
Bases are places where your team can spawn, pick up or reload a weapon, heal up or hide from the enemy. You start with one, and can unlock more and upgrade them throughout the round. Order more weapons, set up traps, construct sniper towers. The responsibility falls on you to secure your position from enemy advances. Feel at home!
On either side, you can choose between a slow, heavily-armored tank your enemies don't want be left alone close with, a light-armored agile runner that no one can escape from, or a classic soldier class in between for those who seek balance and adaptability.
